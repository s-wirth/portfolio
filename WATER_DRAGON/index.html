Water Dragon

Dragons have always been my favourite mythical creature. I wanted to play with the dynamic pose of the serpentine body interacting with the fluid motion of the waves around it. I focused a lot on the details of the water running off the freshly emerged Dragon, as well as the stark contrast of the sun in the background.

Details

  • Materials: Acrylic on Cardboard
  • Dimensions: 40cm x 30cm
